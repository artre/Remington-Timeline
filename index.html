SHOOTING
WORLD RECORD

In October 1959, a Reminton field Rep, Tom Frye, used two weeks of his vacation to brake a fifty-two-year-old shooting record. Alternately using three Remington Nylon 66 rifles, Frye shot at and hit all but six of 100,010 wooden blocks that were tossed in the air by his assistant.
